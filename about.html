Meet The Founder

Owolabi Sambo Isaac

Founder & Sole Owner of Owosam Branding

Owolabi Sambo Isaac is a branding specialist and entrepreneur with a passion for helping businesses build strong identities that attract attention and inspire confidence. He founded Owosam Branding with a vision to deliver top-notch branding and printing services that combine creativity, quality and professionalism.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted name for businesses, organizations and individuals across Lagos who need branding that actually works — from logo design and signage to vehicle wraps and printed materials.

Owolabi is self-taught across both design and web development, and personally oversees every project that comes through Owosam Branding to make sure it meets the same standard he'd want for his own brand.
